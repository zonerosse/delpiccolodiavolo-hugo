  <div class="lead"><strong>In brief:</strong> While Staffordshire Bull Terriers are NOT the same as "pit bulls," they can be subject to misidentification and restrictions. Understanding the legal landscape protects you and your dog.</div>
  <h2>Italy: Current status</h2>
  <ul>
  <li>No breed-specific dangerous dog lists since 2009</li>
  <li>All dogs assessed individually on behavior</li>
  <li>Liability insurance strongly recommended</li>
  <li>Leash and muzzle rules apply to all breeds in public spaces</li>
  </ul>
  <h2>Travel considerations</h2>
  <ul>
  <li><strong>UK:</strong> Staffies are legal and popular</li>
  <li><strong>Germany:</strong> Check local Bundesland regulations</li>
  <li><strong>France:</strong> Staffies are Category 2 - restrictions apply</li>
  <li>Always research destination country before traveling</li>
  </ul>
  <h2>Protecting yourself</h2>
  <ul>
  <li>Keep pedigree and registration documents accessible</li>
  <li>Carry vaccination records with microchip number</li>
  <li>Have liability insurance</li>
  <li>Know the difference between Staffy and restricted breeds</li>
  </ul>
