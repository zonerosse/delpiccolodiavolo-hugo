  <div class="lead"><strong>In brief:</strong> Different bloodlines within the Staffordshire Bull Terrier breed show variation in type. Understanding these helps when choosing a puppy and communicating with breeders.</div>
  <h2>Irish lines</h2>
  <ul>
  <li>Generally more compact and heavier-boned</li>
  <li>Broader heads, more pronounced cheek muscles</li>
  <li>Known lines: Doyle's, Donoghue's legacy</li>
  </ul>
  <h2>English lines</h2>
  <ul>
  <li>Often slightly more elegant and athletic</li>
  <li>Emphasis on movement and overall balance</li>
  <li>Strong showing tradition</li>
  </ul>
  <h2>What matters most</h2>
  <ul>
  <li>Health testing regardless of line</li>
  <li>Temperament true to breed character</li>
  <li>Structure that allows healthy, pain-free movement</li>
  <li>Breeder's knowledge and commitment</li>
  </ul>
  <div class="callout"><p class="callout-title">üêæ Breeder's advice</p>
